About Us

Who We Are

ZapAround is on a mission to make travel planning effortless. Our AI-driven platform curates personalised itineraries so you can spend less time researching and more time experiencing the world.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a team of travel enthusiasts and technologists, ZapAround was born out of frustration with the fragmented, time-consuming planning process. We imagined a smarter way—one that understands your interests, finds the best places, and organises everything in minutes.

Today, thousands of travellers use ZapAround to discover hidden gems, collaborate with friends, and book with confidence.

Mission

To empower every traveller with AI-powered tools that turn dreams into well-crafted journeys.

Vision

A world where planning a trip is as delightful as taking it—seamless, personalised, and inspiring.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We push boundaries with cutting-edge technology.
  • Community: Travel is better together—collaboration is at our core.
  • Sustainability: We promote responsible choices that respect our planet.
  • Delight: We obsess over creating joyful, intuitive experiences.

Why Choose ZapAround?

  • Smart recommendations tailored to your style and budget.
  • Real-time updates, maps, and collaboration tools in one place.
  • Trusted by thousands with a 4.9/5 average user rating.
